Types of telescopes

Refractor Telescopes

  • Fairly portable telescope form
  • Sturdy build
  • Low maintenance
  • Color fringing may occur
  • Relatively expensive

Relfector Telescopes

  • Beginner-friendly
  • Cost the least
  • Able to view smaller objects
  • Can get very large
  • Need to use a separate red dot finder

Catadioptric Telescopes

  • Most portable type of telescope
  • May require collimation
